About Ollie

Expert Hands. Deep Knowledge. Pacific Northwest Roots.

With over 13 years of experience and advanced training in sports and neuromuscular therapy, Ollie Tripp brings a rare combination of clinical expertise and intuitive bodywork to every session.

Originally honing his craft in Jackson Hole, Wyoming — serving athletes, skiers, and active adults who demand the best from their bodies — Ollie now brings that same level of care to the outdoor-loving community of Bellingham, WA.
